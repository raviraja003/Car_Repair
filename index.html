Frequently Asked Questions

Why should you choose GoMechanic Jaipur for your car service?

Your car deserves nothing but the best car repair and services in town. GoMechanic provides you with exactly that at much cheaper prices You can lavishly select from a wide array of car service packages which come with a maximum quality assurance and service warranty.

How extensively are GoMechanic car service centres spread in Jaipur?

GoMechanic boasts of more than 15 well equipped and easy-to-reach car service workshops in Jaipur. While booking a package online, your car is assigned to the nearest GoMechanic Jaipur facility for your convenience.

What car repair and service packages does GoMechanic offer in Jaipur?

You can get your regular car service, make it spotless and dent less with our car rubbing/polishing service, get your car tyres rotated or give your car a complete cleaning experience and much more. Visit the GoMechanic website to find the complete list of services offered.

What are the different ways to book a car repair package from GoMechanic in Jaipur?

You can go the regular way by visiting the nearest GoMechanic Jaipur centre or calling us at 8658340970. Or you can go the GoMechanic way and book a car service through the website and Android App.

What are the charges for a basic car service at GoMechanic Jaipur?

A bare minimum car service starts at Rs.1999/- All GOMechanic price tags are about 40% off compared to standard centre charges. For expenses related to all other service packages in Jaipur, please visit the GoMechanic website.

Does GoMechanic car repair come with a warranty?

Yes. To keep your car repair and its aftermath free of hassles, GoMechanic provides a month/1000km service warranty on all its services.
